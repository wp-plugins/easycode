== Description ==

* It can help you to show your codes in a better way.
* It has some functions to help people to read codes easilier.
* You can choose a style that fits your blog.

<p>After installation,you can put your codes into your words.</p>
Please write:<br>
[code]<br>
* a ordered list (&lt;ol&gt;) *<br>
[/code]<br>

HTML:<br>
[code]<br>
&lt;ol&gt;<br>
 &lt;li&gt;Some Codes&lt;/li&gt;<br>
 &lt;li&gt;Some Codes&lt;/li&gt;<br>
 бнбн
&lt;/ol&gt;
[/code]

So Easy!
And you can also change options in Setting Center.
